# Blender Career World Visual Review

## Before State

The previous generated models read as blockouts rather than authored Blender models.

- Environment: the island is a mostly rectangular slab with straight bar paths, sparse props and several trees/rocks that read as detached from the designed world.
- Filmstaden: the red marquee is a giant tunnel-like half cylinder, windows are visibly detached blocks, facade depth is weak, and the sign dominates the model instead of integrating with the canopy.
- Visma: the three building volumes are present but read as separate blocks; window rectangles float in front of walls and the entrance lacks believable depth and stair/canopy structure.
- Sodra: the curved facade exists but roof slabs and pavilion blocks float or clip. The facade lacks a coherent grid and structural connection.
- Dasa: the harvester silhouette is too box-based. Body, cabin, boom and head are not structurally connected enough, and wheel/axle proportions are weak.
- Education: the school has brick massing but no convincing roofline, inconsistent window placement, detached windows and a central entrance that reads as a blockout.

## Rebuild Direction

The rebuilt pass must prioritize silhouette, ground contact and connected architectural assemblies over raw detail. Every visible window should be embedded in a frame on a wall, roof slabs should sit on building bodies, paths should be connected curves on terrain, and the Dasa boom/head should visibly connect to the chassis.

The target style is a stylized Nordic architectural miniature with coherent materials, soft bevels, restrained colors and readable forms from the Three.js hero camera.
