# Blender Career World Visual Review - Pass 2

Date: 2026-06-25

## Environment

The island is still read as a flat presentation board. Five rectangular grass pads reveal the landmark slots instead of feeling like terrain zones, and paths are short radial spokes with hard junctions. The Career Hub is too cylindrical and simple. Trees remain cone-like and several landscape clusters feel decorative rather than grounded into a terrain composition. The mobile preview leaves too much empty background; the island should fill more of the frame.

## Dasa Forestry

The harvester is recognizable but still constructed from rounded boxes. The cabin reads as a black block instead of an angled operator cab, tire volume and hubs are too simple, and the harvester head is still a compact block. The boom is connected, but joints, cylinders and hoses need stronger mechanical hierarchy. The forest floor exists, but trees are still toy-like.

## Education

The roof is visibly floating because the script placed roof geometry above the actual wall top. Side blocks and decorative verticals feel separate from the central school mass. The entrance reads as a circular badge on a wall, not as a recessed arched architectural doorway. Windows are aligned, but facade depth, cornice, plinth and roof structure need to make the building feel physically complete.

## Filmstaden

The facade is recognizable, but the roof cornice and dark cap float above the building for the same height-placement reason. The marquee is much closer to the reference than the earlier blockout, but it needs support columns, underside thickness and stronger facade attachment. The entrance and poster cases are too shallow and need a more believable cinema forecourt.

## Sodra

The curved glass facade is the strongest asset. The remaining problem is structural connection: roof, roof deck, pavilion and some fins still read as placed above the building rather than supported by it. The upper pavilion needs to sit on the roof deck with visible supports, and the curved roof should follow the main facade more deliberately.

## Visma

The orange core and white wings are readable, but roofline and parapet are visibly floating above the building volumes. The entrance canopy, sign and steps need clearer physical attachment to the facade and ground. The window grid is consistent, but projection depth should be restrained and the central atrium needs stronger vertical structure.

## References Used

- Filmstaden reference: brick facade, shallow connected red marquee, stone ground floor, red-framed entrance and mounted circular sign.
- Visma reference: connected white side wings, orange central core, central glass atrium, canopy, steps and landscaping.
- Sodra reference: curved glass headquarters, white structural grid, connected side wings, roof overhang and pavilion on roof.
- Dasa reference: articulated green forestry harvester, angled glass cab, large rubber tires, black hydraulic boom and compact harvester head in pine forest.
- Education reference: historic red-brick school with continuous roofline, central arched entrance, stone base, repeated tall windows and decorative brick roof details.
